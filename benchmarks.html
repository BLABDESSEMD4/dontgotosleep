GLOBAL BENCHMARKS

Giants who paved the way for a circular future.

Reflow

The Innovators

Started with a mission to turn plastic waste from developing countries into high-quality 3D filament. They began by working directly with waste collectors in Nairobi to create a social-impact supply chain.

❝

Transforms waste into creative potential, mirroring our filament production goals.

Precious Plastic

The Community

Started in 2013 as a graduation project by Dave Hakkens. It was built on the idea of "Open Source" — sharing machine designs for free so anyone, anywhere, can start their own recycling workshop.

❝

Democratizes recycling technology, inspiring our community-driven approach.

Filamentive

Filamentive

The Quality Standard

Founded to address the massive amount of plastic waste generated by 3D printing itself. They started by researching how to make recycled filament that performs exactly like "virgin" plastic.

❝

Proves that recycled materials can meet industrial quality standards.

Umicore

Umicore

The Industrial Giant

Originally a mining company with over 100 years of history. They made a massive strategic pivot to become a world leader in "Urban Mining" — extracting precious metals (like copper) from old electronics and wires.

❝

A model for pivot and scale: from traditional mining to urban mining.

Closing the Loop

Closing the Loop

The Service Model

Started with a simple "One-for-One" concept. For every new phone a company buys, Closing the Loop collects and recycles one scrap phone from a country where waste is a problem, making the industry "waste-neutral."

❝

Innovative business models that fund waste collection in developing nations.
